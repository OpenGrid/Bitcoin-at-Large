Bitcoin Legality, Taxation, etc. by Country(note)

Country Legality Taxation Classification Exchanges Sources/etc.
United States Inaction by the federal government, and two positive senate hearings has led to a de-facto legality of Bitcoin. Bitcoin miners do not need to register as money transmitters. No official statement from the IRS. Mining may be viewed as income. Depending on the length of time a bitcoin was held, it may be taxed as short/long term capital gains. See Bitcoin Magazine Awaiting IRS clarification Local Bitcoins, Coinbase, CampBX, Kraken FinCEN, Bitcoin Magazine, Senate Hearings
China Deemed not a currency. Restricted banks from using Bitcoin as a currency. No third party payment processors can process transactions involving digital currencies. BTC China, Huobi New York Times 1, 2
Denmark Likely legal Bloomberg
France Exchanges can operate as real banks. The Bank of France has warned people of the risks. Bitcoin-Central Reuters, Bitcointalk Thread
Germany Deemed "units of account" Same as regional currencies, Bitcoin sales are subject to sales tax German Article
United Kingdom Legal. Exchanges do not need to register.
Canada Legal Taxed as income tax and capital gains. Similar to a commodity Canadian Virtual Exchange, Coinkite Government Site
Norway Legal, not a valid currency Government has stated it is not a currency, but an asset that will be taxed via capital gains. Generic Asset, see previous cell Justcoin Market Watch
India Unknown. Not supported or regulated by the government. The Reserve Bank of India has warned and cautioned users of virtual currencies of the associated risks. BuySellBitCo.in (trading halted) Reserve Bank of India, Times of India, No plans to regulate
Australia The Australian Taxation office will clarify their stance February 2014 Australian Tax Office
Belgium Legal. Finance minister "has no problem with Bitcoin." Coindesk
Switzerland Undecided. Proposal to treat Bitcoin as a foreign currency. Coindesk
Poland Legal Polish Site
Slovenia Legal Some income will be taxed, corporate taxes are unclear. Bitcoin sold for profit are not taxed as capital gains. Bitstamp Coindesk on tax, Tax Release
Japan Mt. Gox
Bulgaria BTC-E
Thailand Implicitly illegal Trading Suspended, techinasia.com
